Michigan Club Basketball

We are the University of Michigan Men's basketball club team. We currently compete in the NCBBA league in which we compete in games and tournaments agent other Divsion 1 club teams. Practices are 2-3 times a week and tryouts are held every fall.

We are always looking to add new prospective mememebrs to the family! If you are interested please fill out the attached form We take players of all ages from freshman to Grad students. Our only requirment is that each player is taking a full course load while playing. Before our tryouts we also hold annual open gyms for a 5 follar entry fee. these open gyms give prospective memebers the opportunity to meet the Team as well as enjoy some high-leveled basketball!
